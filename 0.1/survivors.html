The following is a list of each possible survivor class and their recommended playstyle:

Scout

Zombie Scouts can only use the Sandman. When a launched ball hits a survivor, all survivors within a certain radius are inflicted with bleeding damage for a short amount of time.

Soldier

Zombie Soldiers cannot use the Pain Train, Market Gardener or Escape Plan.

Pyro

Zombie Pyros cannot use the Axtinguisher or Neon Annihilator.

Demoman

Zombie Demomen can only use the Bottle, Scotsman's Skullcutter and Half-Zatoichi.

Heavy

Zombie Heavies cannot use the Gloves of Running Urgently, Eviction Notice or Holliday Punch.

Engineer

Zombie Engineers are forced to use the Jag. They can build, but none of the buildings are allowed to be upgraded, repaired or have their construction sped up, and they start only being able to build Teleporters. Because ammo and health pickups are inaccessible to zombie players, zombie Engineers have passive metal regeneration.

Medic

Zombie Medics cannot use the Amputator, and their Ubercharge is always 0%.

Sniper

Zombie Snipers cannot use the Bushwacka.

Spy

Zombies Spies cannot use the Your Eternal Reward or Conniver's Kunai. They can disguise and cloak with a reduced cloak time, but backstabs will stun the enemy and reduce their health by a set amount comparable to a facestab.
